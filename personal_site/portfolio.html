Courtyard Escape!

This was a final project for an intro to 3D Computer Graphics. The premise of the game is that there are two players sharing a screen and each person gets 5 seconds of screen time before the other person's point of view swaps in. Player 1 is trying to find the key in the Courtyard and then escape through a hidden door, while player 2 is trying to corner and capture player 1. Player 1 controls consist of the standard WASD, and player 2 controls are arrow keys.
